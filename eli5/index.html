Step by step

What happens when you use it

A typical conversation, broken down into plain English.

1

You say something

You type a message — or speak, or send it via Telegram — asking Hermes to do something. "Summarise my emails." "Write me a Python script." "What's the weather in Tokyo?"

2

Hermes checks its memory

Before answering, Hermes looks up what it knows about you — your preferences, past conversations, files you've shared, tasks you've set. It doesn't start from zero every time.

3

It picks the best AI model and gets to work

Hermes sends your request to a powerful AI model (Claude, GPT, Gemini — you choose). The model reasons through the answer, uses tools if needed (searching the web, running code), and drafts a reply.

4

You get the answer, wherever you are

The reply lands in whatever app you're already using. Telegram, Discord, your browser — Hermes meets you where you are. No app switching required.

5

It saves what matters and keeps going

Hermes updates its memory with anything worth keeping. If you set a recurring task ("check my portfolio every morning"), it runs that on a schedule — even while you sleep.

The three things that make it special

Why not just use ChatGPT?

Good question. Here's what Hermes does that standalone AI chat apps don't.

🧠

It actually remembers you

Most AI forgets everything the moment you close the tab. Hermes keeps a persistent memory of who you are and what you care about — across every conversation, forever.

⏰

It works while you sleep

You can schedule tasks. "Check Hacker News every morning and tell me the three most interesting posts." Hermes does it automatically. No reminders, no opening an app.

📲

It goes where you go

Telegram, Discord, WhatsApp, email — Hermes talks to you in whatever app you already live in. One assistant, everywhere you are.

🔧

It can actually do things

Not just talk — it can run code, search the web, edit files, call APIs. Hermes has tools. Real tools. It's a full agent, not just a chatbot.

🔒

It runs on your machine

Hermes lives on your own computer. Your conversations stay private. You control what AI model it uses and where your data goes. No company owns your history.

🤖

Best model, your choice

Claude, GPT, Gemini, DeepSeek — bring your own API keys and use whichever model you like best. Switch anytime. No vendor lock-in.
